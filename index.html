Swiss striker Embolo refuses to celebrate goal
against Cameroon


Reuters. AL WAKRAH
Thu Nov 24, 2022 05:31 PM Last update on: Thu Nov 24, 2022 05:39 P



Photo: Reuters
Switzerland striker Breel Embolo scored against the land of his birth
Cameroon in their World Cup Group G clash at the Al Janoub Stadium on
Thursday but refused to celebrate and looked almost apologetic as his
team mates rushed to congratulate him.

Embolo swept home a Xherdan Shaqiri cross to give the
Swiss the lead in the 48th minute after they struggled to break the deadlock in the first half and
were second best in the game, which was 0-0 at the break.

The 25-year-old striker, who was born in Yaounde but raised in Basel, stood
still with an apologetic frown on his face after he scored while the
Switzerland players celebrated around him.

The goal was a first shot on target for the Swiss, who had battled to contain
the African side in the opening 45 minutes.
